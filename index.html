About Us

ENGINEERING -another sublime manifestation of “VigyanBharati Charitable Trust” is one of the Top Engineering Institutes in Bhubaneswar.

With the vision to emerge as a premier Technical institution at National level, the institute came into existence in the year 2009 and has made bottomless efforts to achieve its mission of imparting quality education of International standards in the minds of students. This Best Engineering College in Bhubaneswar is situated on the banks of river Kuakhai that builds up a spectacular view filled up with beautiful surroundings where students can gather competitive skills in a green and serene ambience. The sprawling campus, being an outstanding house of natural beauty located in the heart of the city sets up a perfect platform for teaching-learning.
